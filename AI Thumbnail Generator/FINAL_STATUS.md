5. Auto-redirects to home with welcome message

### ✅ Login
1. Click "Get Started"  
2. Fill login form
3. Click "Login"
4. Auto-redirects to home with welcome message

### ✅ Generate Thumbnail
1. Click "Generate"
2. Enter title (required)
3. Select style
4. Click "Generate Thumbnail"
5. Wait 20-60 seconds
6. Image appears with download button

### ✅ View History
1. Click "My Generations"
2. See all your thumbnails
3. Click "New Thumbnail" to create more

### ✅ Logout
1. Click "Logout" button in navbar
2. Redirects to home
3. Cannot access Generate/My Generations
